1. You are expected to install a 5 node Hadoop cluster (use HDP quickstart). The cluster 

requirements are

	a. It should be Highly Available

	b. It should be a multi tenant cluster. set up Fair scheduler. It should have two pools  

	‘dev’ and ‘admin’, with development having 75 % weights and admin having the 

	rest 25 %. Your cluster should have the first names of your team members as its 

	users. You are free to allot them the pools you want.

	c. Run Hive, Pig, Sqoop, and Spark on your clusters.

	d. Run YARN (MRv2) on the cluster.

2. You are free to choose where to run your namenode, datanodes, and other processes 

run.
